Who are we ?

E-Cell, NSIT

E-Cell NSIT is a non profit organisation run by students of NSIT that aims at manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of young students. We at E-Cell NSIT host various workshops, speaker sessions, innovative games, and competitions for aspiring entreprenuers all throughout the year and support them by providing necessary resources such as mentorship, consultancy, networking and angel support.
